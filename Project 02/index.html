Chocolate Chip

---

2/3 cup salted butter

½ cup sugar

½ cup brown sugar

1 egg

1 tsp vanilla

2 tsp hot water

½ tsp baking soda

½ tsp salt

1 ½ cups flour

½ cup walnuts

6oz chocolate chips

---

Bake at 375 °F for 10-12 minutes.

Gingersnaps

---

1 cup salted butter

1 cup sugar

1 egg

¼ cup molasses

2 tsp baking soda

1 tsp ground cinnamon

1 tsp ground cloves

1 tsp ground ginger

¼ tsp salt

Additional sugar to roll dough balls in before baking

---

Bake at 375 °F for 10 minutes or untill surface cracks.

Jam Thumbprint

---

2/3 cup salted butter

½ cup sugar

2 egg yolks

1 tsp vanilla

1 ½ cup sifted flour

2 slightly beaten egg whites

¾ cup finely chopped walnuts

1/3 cup strawberry or rasberry preserves

---

Shape in ¾ inch balls dip in egg whites, then roll in nuts. Press down centers with thumb. Bake 350 °F for 15-17 minutes. Fill with preserves once cooled

Snickerdoodles

---

1 ¼ cup salted butter

2 ½ cup sugar

2 eggs

1 ½ tsp vanilla

½ tsp lemon extract/p>

1 ¼ cups flour

2 tsp baking powder

1 tsp baking soda

¾ tsp salt

1 cup buttermilk

2 tsp cinnamon

---

Cream butter and 2 cups sugar; add eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Blend in extracts; set aside. In another mixing bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt; add alternately to sugar mixture with buttermilk. Cover and chill dough for 4 hours; shape quarter-cup measures of dough into balls. Roll in remaining sugar and cinnamon mixture; gently press balls ½ inch thick. Bake at 375 °F for 15 minutes

Italian Rainbow Cookies

---

4 large eggs separated

1 cup sugar

8 oz almond paste

1 ¼ cups unsalted butter

1 tsp almond extract/p>

2 cups flour

½ tsp Kosher salt

red food coloring/p>

green food coloring

1 cup buttermilk

10 ounces raspberry and/ or apricot preserves

7 ounces semisweet chocolate

---

Prepare the sheet pans. Grease, flour, and line the bottoms of three 9×13 with parchment paper. Beat egg whites until they just hold stiff peaks. Mix sugar and almond paste. Incorporate butter. Fold in egg whites. Divide batter among 3 bowls. Color each tray with food coloring. Bake 8-10 Minutes at 350 °F. Assemble Layers with perserves between. Cover top with melted semisweet chocolate. Chill and cut to serve.
